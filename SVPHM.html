Welcome to St Vincent's Private Hospital Melbourne

For over 100 years, St Vincent's Private Hospital Melbourne has excelled in the delivery of quality patient care. We work in partnership with our patients and their families to take a compassionate and holistic approach to healthcare.

Our experienced team of clinical professionals, care givers and support staff are equipped to provide the highest level of therapeutic, medical and surgical services. We work together to ensure your safety, well-being and recovery.

St Vincent's Kids

At St Vincent’s Kids we specialise in orthopaedics (bones), ear nose and throat surgery, urology, plastics and general surgery for infants, children and adolescents.

Maternity

At St Vincent's Private Hospital Melbourne, we offer personalised care from our team of highly qualified obstetricians, paediatricians, midwives and nursing staff.

Our Neuroscience Services


St Vincent’s Private Hospital Melbourne is a leader in the speciality of Neuroscience. Our neurosurgeons and neurologists treat patients for a wide range of neurological disorders.
